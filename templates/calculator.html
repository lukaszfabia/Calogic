Help

accepted expressions

  • factorial - x!
  • square root - sqrt(x)
  • cube root - cbrt(x)
  • floor (integer part) - floor(x)
  • round-up - ceil(x)
  • absolute value - |x|
  • inversion - 1/x
  • power - x^n
  • n-root - nrt(x,n)
